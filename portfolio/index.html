about me

Who I Am

I'm a Network Engineer with hands-on experience designing, implementing, and troubleshooting enterprise-grade network infrastructure. My roots are in traditional networking — routers, switches, firewalls, and routing protocols — and I've been expanding into automation and DevOps to make operations faster and more reliable.

I'm actively building skills in network automation with Python, Ansible, and Terraform, while deepening my knowledge in cybersecurity — from network hardening to threat detection and incident response. I'm also fascinated by AI for network operations: using machine learning to detect anomalies, predict failures, and streamline network management.

My goal is to grow into a role where I can combine networking expertise with modern engineering practices to help organizations build resilient, automated, and secure infrastructure.
